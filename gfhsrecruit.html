This is what we build the robots for. View the game video for a quick overview. If you want more detail, see the game manual.

There is a new style of game this year. It's AI based. This means more programming... A LOT more programming. I aim to have an AI team, but there should be still "old school" teams.

VEX AI Video

Don't think you can do it? Think again!

Are you in...

Mr. Baker's class? Advanced Computer Math alone gives you all the programming knowledge you wil need!

A PLTW class? Skills you learn in those classes are often applied here.

A math class? A problem-solving mindset is a very handy thing to have.

An arts class? Being able to reflect ideas or concepts on paper is crucial!

An english class? Just being able to write in a notebook is a role that is often overlooked, but is SO IMPORTANT!!

If you don't think you'll do well because you're "not smart" or "don't know anything about it", that's alright! We can teach you the proper knowledge and skills.

What To Expect...

Meetings every Tuesday and Thursday, with competitions on the weekends occasionally

Intense competitions that require skill, focus, strategy, and effort

Working hands-on with real robot parts... this isn't LEGO!

A utilisation of various tools, ranging from power tools to hand tools

An engineering notebook that needs to be written in each meeting

This will run at least until about March, but can go farther if you put the work in to go to Worlds!

Overall, you can expect out of robotics only as much as you put in. If you do your best, you can and will have a successful year.
